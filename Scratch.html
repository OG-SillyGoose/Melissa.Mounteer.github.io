My Nail Art Process

  1. Prep & Shape
    1. File and buff the nail surface
    2. Push back cuticles
    3. Apply Dehydrator and Primer
    4. Apply base coat
  2. Application
    • Apply color in thin, even layers
    • Add hand-painted details with a fine brush
    • Add other embellishments; stickers, foils, gems, etc.
  3. Finish
    1. Clean up edges
    2. Seal with top coat
    3. Apply and massage cuticle oil on hands
